Taco Recipe

Traditional Mexican beef tacos are made with marinated sliced or shredded beef on soft corn tortillas. Those are great! But, thatâ€™s not what most Americans think of when we think of classic tacos.

Ingredients

  • Marinated Beef
  • Onions
  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa Roja
  • oil

Steps

  1. Cook the marinated beef over a flat top grill with oil
  2. Add the tortillas to the grill and oil them up while heating them up
  3. Make sure your tortillas has extra flavor from all the seasoning and marinated beef
  4. take your beef and put it on the tortilla
  5. take both your onion and cilantro and put them over the beef
  6. add your salsa of choice
  7. ENJOY!
